  • Tokenomics — the topic of understanding the supply and demand characteristics of cryptocurrency. In the traditional economy, economists monitor the issuance of a currency using official money supply data. The numbers they report are generally called M1, M2 and — depending upon the country — M3 or M4 as well. An in-depth explanation of the four M categories is beyond this tokenomics analysis: just know that M1 is a measurement of the most liquid monies, M2 is less liquid, and so on. These numbers help to enable transparency and monitoring of different aspects of the supply of a currency.
